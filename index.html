Good to know

Is it really a one-time payment?

Yes. Pay once, download the installer, and it's yours — including future updates. There is no subscription and no account to cancel.

Where do my recordings go?

Straight to a folder on your own computer that you choose. BloomRecorder makes zero network calls — nothing is uploaded, ever.

Can I record my screen and webcam at the same time?

Yes. Choose “Screen + Camera.” Then either save them as two separate files (each with the full audio) or as a single combined picture-in-picture video.

Does it capture audio?

Both your microphone and your computer's system audio, mixed cleanly — and included in every file it saves.

Which platforms are supported?

Windows and macOS, with native installers for each.
